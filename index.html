2016 Heathmont

Photographic Competition and Display!


This year it's the

F.J.C Rogers Reserve

This was the third in the series of local Reserves competitions.

All photos entered have been taken within the wider areas of the Reserve in 2016 and been

of the Reserve it self, its landscape, lant life, nature, or visitors within the Reserve.

Learn more

The F.J.C Rogers Reserve is a very small reserve in Heathmont along Camplbell Street near the Heathmont railway station This parcel of bushland was given his name in 1973. Fred Rogers came to Heathmont in 1945 and among other things, co-founded the Ringwood Field Naturalists Club, wrote many books on native plants and was school headmmaster.
